# AIS_datathon_2026

## Problem Statement
To what extent still the American Dream attainable?
- Is the American Dream attainbale across the U.S. (statewise)?
- Which states are growing the fastest?
- Are incomes rising with GDP?
- Are some regions falling behind?
- Which industry will most likely achieve the American Dream?
- How likely does people in tech achieve the American dream?

## Definition
American dream 
-  Anybody, from any background, can be successful and follow their dreams with enough hard work and perseverance.

- Starting a family in a nice town, with enough financial success to be generally free from worry. The traditional idea of "making it". 

- Better in life than previous generations.
